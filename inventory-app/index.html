SOP Framework

Basic Electronics Lab

All breadboards and Arduino modules must be cleared of components after each session. Trainers conduct a visual check at the end of every class and record status via Individual Entry.

Advanced Prototyping Lab

3D printers and benchtop instruments require a signed logbook entry before use. Never leave running machines unattended. LiPo batteries must be stored in fireproof bags after every session.

Check-out / Check-in

Trainers must log exact quantities given to students using Individual Entry for sensitive or high-value items. For generic consumables, Bulk Entry is permitted.

Damaged & Missing Goods

Items marked Broken must be physically segregated and tagged. Items marked Missing trigger an immediate lab search. All anomalies are auto-reported via Telegram to the Admin.

Export & Records

Each completed audit generates a downloadable report in your chosen format — Excel (.csv) for digital filing or Image (.png) for WhatsApp/print sharing. Audit history is retained locally.
